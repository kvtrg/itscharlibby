What is Dog Bites Riso Studio?

Founded in 2021, Dog Bites Riso Studio was born from a shared love of printmaking, design and innovation. Dog Bites Riso Studio is a Vietnamese-owned print and design studio run by Keith Truong. Dog Bites Riso Studio celebrates Riso printing and works to expand the technical limits and boundaries of the digital duplicator process.



Keith Truong is a multi-disciplinary artist born and raised in Saigon, Vietnam, currently based in Boston, Massachusetts. Keith's work involves digital 3D modeling, graphic design, and print media. They are heavily influenced by cinematic themes and the sequencing of images. They seek to explore identity, relationships, and how varying interpretation can inform and transform both the thematic and technical qualities of their work.
